## Overview

Dive into the vibrant and engaging world of **Chroma Chaos**, an addictive block puzzle game designed for Android. Strategically drop multicolored shape blocks onto the grid to form complete horizontal or vertical lines and clear them for points. Keep the screen clear and aim for the highest score! Be on the lookout for special "jewel" blocks that might introduce unique and exciting gameplay mechanics.

## Features

* **Intuitive Drag-and-Drop Controls:** Easily move and place blocks with simple touch gestures.
* **Variety of Colorful Shapes:** Encounter a range of different block formations to keep the gameplay fresh.
* **Line Clearing Mechanics:** Form complete horizontal or vertical lines to clear blocks and earn points.
* **Potential Special Jewel Blocks:** Discover unique blocks with special abilities that can change the game in exciting ways (e.g., bombs, color clears, score multipliers - *implementation dependent*).
* **Score Tracking:** Keep track of your high scores and challenge yourself to improve.
* **Clean and Engaging User Interface:** Enjoy a visually appealing and easy-to-understand game interface.
* **[Potentially] Endless Gameplay:** See how long you can last and how high you can score before the grid fills up.
* **[Potentially] Offline Play:** Enjoy Chroma Chaos anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection.

## How to Play

1.  **Drag and Drop:** Touch and drag the presented multicolored block to your desired horizontal position on the grid.
2.  **Rotate (if implemented):** Tap or use a designated gesture to rotate the block to the desired orientation before dropping.
3.  **Drop:** Release the block to let it fall into place on the grid.
4.  **Clear Lines:** Once a complete horizontal or vertical line of blocks is formed, it will automatically clear, and you'll earn points.
5.  **Game Over:** The game ends when a new block cannot be placed on the grid because the top rows are full.
